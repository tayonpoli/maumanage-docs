This section provides a clear, structured **step-by-step guide** for registering a new user account on your MauManage application. Follow these steps to create your account and start managing your business.

## Steps

To register a new user account, follow these steps:

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Go to the Sign-Up Link">
    Navigate to the **Sign-up link** to begin the registration process.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Input User Details">
    Enter your **User name** and **email address** into the respective fields.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Set Password">
    Input your desired **password** and then **confirm the password** in the designated fields.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Click Sign Up">
    Click on the "**Sign Up**" button to complete your registration.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
