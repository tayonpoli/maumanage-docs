This section provides a clear, structured **step-by-step guide** for editing company profile within your MauManage application.

## Steps

To edit an company profile, follow these steps:

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Navigate to Profile Menu">
    Go to the **Profile menu** within the MauManage application.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Edit Company">
    Click on "**Edit**" button in the top-right of company information card.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Modify Fields">
    Change the necessary **fields** in the edit company profile form.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Update Company Profile">
    Click "**Update**" to submit the form and save the changes to the company profile.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
