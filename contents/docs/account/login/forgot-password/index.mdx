This section provides a clear, structured **step-by-step guide** for resetting your password for the MauManage application, helping you regain access to your account.

## Steps

To reset your password, follow these steps:

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Navigate to Login Page">
    Go to the **login page** of the MauManage ERP application.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Access Forgot Password Link">
    Click on the "**Forgot Password**" link, located upon the password field.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Enter Registered Email">
    Input your **registered email address** in the provided field to receive a password reset link.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Check Your Email">
    Open the email from MauManage and click on the **password reset link** or enter the provided verification code.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Set New Password">
    Enter and confirm your **new password** in the designated fields.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Login with New Password">
    Return to the login page and sign in using your newly set password.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
