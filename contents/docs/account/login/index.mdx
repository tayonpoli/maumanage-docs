This section provides a clear, structured step-by-step guide for logging into your MauManage application.

## Steps

To login to our application, follow these steps:

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Open the ERP Login Page">
    Navigate to the **login page** of the MauManage ERP application.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Enter Credentials">
    Input your **email address** and **password** into the respective fields.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Click Login Button">
    Click on the "**Login**" button to proceed.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Access Dashboard">
    Upon successful login, your **dashboard** will be displayed, providing access to the system's features.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
