This section provides a clear, structured **step-by-step guide** for viewing sales orders and their detailed information within your MauManage application.

## Steps

To view sales orders, follow these steps:

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Navigate to Sales Menu">
    Go to the **Sales menu** within the MauManage application.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Review Sales Statistics">
    You will see the **current sales statistics** displayed.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="View Sales Order List">
    You can then view the **list of sales orders** in the table below.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="View Order Details">
    To see the full details of a specific sales order, click on "**View Details**" in the corresponding row in the table.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
