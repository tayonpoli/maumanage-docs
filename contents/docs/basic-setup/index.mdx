## MauManage

**MauManage** is a web-based system that helps manage business operations such as sales, purchasing, production, and inventory in one place
and also equipped with Al technology for business analysis.

The interface is simple and easy to use, and can be accessed directly through a browser without the need for additional installation.

## Why use MauManage?

This web-based ERP system designed to integrate your business processes and help manage business operations more efficiently.

Use it for:

- **Simplify Recording & Tracking Transactions** – Streamlines data entry processes and easy tracking of all transactions
- **Improve Work Efficiency** – With automation and integration, operational tasks become faster and more efficient
- **Full Visibility of Entire Business Process** – Provides a clear and comprehensive view of the entire business value chain
- **Automated Reporting** – Generates sales and purchase reports automatically
- **Analyze Your Business Easily** – Built-in analytical tools allow users to easily understand business performance

Integrates all the business process and match with your brand or workflow—no lock-in.

## Core Features

| Feature                              | Description                                                                 |
| ------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Sales**            | Manages the entire sales process from order creation and payment input to reporting, including input via a POS System with receipt printing.           |
| **Purchasing**              | Manages the purchasing process from order creation and payment input to tracking and managing purchase orders.                 |
| **Expenses**                     | Detailed information regarding the "Expenses" feature is not available in the provided documents.                         |
| **Inventory**                           | Manages stock with features to view inventory lists, add new items, adjust quantities, and edit or delete existing items.                          |
| **Production**                | Manages Bill of Materials (BOMs) including viewing lists, creating new ones, and managing (editing/deleting) existing BOMs.                               |
| **AI Analysis**                    | Utilizes AI to forecast sales trends and provides insights from sales and inventory data.                                    |
| **Reporting**                     | Generates sales and purchase reports automatically from the dashboard, and allows for printing or downloading reports.                        |
