This section provides a clear, structured **step-by-step guide** for generating various reports (e.g., sales and purchase reports) within your MauManage application.

## Steps

To generate reports, follow these steps:

<Tabs defaultValue="js" className="pt-5 pb-1">
  <TabsList className="">
    <TabsTrigger value="js">Sales</TabsTrigger>
    <TabsTrigger value="ts">Purchase</TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>
  <TabsContent value="js">

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Navigate to Sales Section">
    Go to the **Sales menu** available within MauManage.
  </StepItem>

 <StepItem title="Select Time Period">
    Select the desired sales transactions **time period**.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Generate Report">
    Click on the "**Generate Report**" button.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Download Report">
    You can then choose to "**Download**" the report to your device.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
  </TabsContent>

  <TabsContent value="ts">
<Step>
  <StepItem title="Navigate to Purchase Section">
    Go to the **Purchase menu** available within MauManage.
  </StepItem>

 <StepItem title="Select Time Period">
    Select the desired purchase transactions **time period**.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Generate Report">
    Click on the "**Generate Report**" button.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Download Report">
    You can then choose to "**Download**" the report to your device.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>
