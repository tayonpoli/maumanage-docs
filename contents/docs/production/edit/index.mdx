This section provides a clear, structured **step-by-step guide** for editing existing Bill of Materials (BOMs) within your MauManage application's Production module.

## Steps

To edit a Bill of Material (BOM), follow these steps:

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Navigate to Production Menu">
    Go to the **Production menu** within the MauManage application.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Select BOM to Edit">
    Click on "**Edit**" in the row of the Bill of Material (BOM) you wish to modify.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Modify Fields">
    Change the necessary **fields** in the Bill of Material (BOM) form.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Update BOM">
    Click "**Update**" to submit the form and save the changes to the Bill of Material.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
