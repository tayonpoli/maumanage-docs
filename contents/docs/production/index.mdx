This section provides a clear, structured **step-by-step guide** for viewing Bill of Materials (BOMs) and their detailed information within your MauManage application's Production module.

## Steps

To view Bill of Materials (BOMs), follow these steps:

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Navigate to Production Menu">
    Go to the **Production menu** within the MauManage application.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="View Bill of Material List">
    You can then view the **list of Bill of Materials (BOMs)** in the table.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="View BOM Details">
    To see the full details of a specific Bill of Material (BOM), click on "**View Details**" in the corresponding row in the table.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
