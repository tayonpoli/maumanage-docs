This section provides a clear, structured **step-by-step guide** for deleting Bill of Materials (BOMs) from your MauManage application's Production module. Follow these steps to remove product structures.

## Steps

To delete a Bill of Material (BOM), follow these steps:

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Navigate to Production Menu">
    Go to the **Production menu** within the MauManage application.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Select BOM to Delete">
    Locate the Bill of Material (BOM) you wish to delete and click on "**Delete**" in its corresponding row.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Confirm Deletion">
    Confirm the deletion in the pop-up dialog.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
