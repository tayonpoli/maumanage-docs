This section provides a clear, structured **step-by-step guide** for creating new Bill of Materials (BOMs) within your MauManage application's Production module.

## Steps

To create a new Bill of Material (BOM), follow these steps:

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Navigate to Production Menu">
    Go to the **Production menu** within the MauManage application.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Initiate New BOM Creation">
    Click on the "**Create New Bill of Material (BOM)**" button.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Input Required Fields">
    Enter all the **necessary information** in the fields provided in the form for the new BOM.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Submit New BOM">
    Click "**Create**" to submit the form and add the new Bill of Material to the system.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
