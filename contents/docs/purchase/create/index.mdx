This section provides a clear, structured **step-by-step guide** for creating new purchase orders within your MauManage application.

## Steps

To create a new purchase order, follow these steps:

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Navigate to Purchasing Menu">
    Go to the **Purchasing menu** within the MauManage application.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Initiate New Purchase Order">
    Click on the "**Create New Purchase**" button.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Input Required Fields">
    Enter all the **necessary information** in the fields provided in the form.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Submit Purchase Order">
    Click "**Create**" to submit the form and add the new purchase order to the system.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
