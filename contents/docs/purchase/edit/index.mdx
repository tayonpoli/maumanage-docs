This section provides a clear, structured **step-by-step guide** for editing existing purchase orders within your MauManage application.

## Steps

To edit a purchase order, follow these steps:

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Navigate to Purchasing Menu">
    Go to the **Purchasing menu** within the MauManage application.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Select Order to Edit">
    Click on "**Edit**" in the row of the purchase order you wish to modify.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Modify Fields">
    Change the necessary **fields** in the purchase order form.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Update Purchase Order">
    Click "**Update**" to submit the form and save the changes to the purchase order.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
