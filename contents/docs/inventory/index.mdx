This section provides a clear, structured **step-by-step guide** for viewing inventory items and their detailed information within your MauManage application.

## Steps

To view inventory items, follow these steps:

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Navigate to Inventory Menu">
    Go to the **Inventory menu** within the MauManage application.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Review Inventory Statistics">
    You will see the **current inventory statistics** displayed.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="View Inventory List">
    You can then view the **list of inventory items** in the table below.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="View Item Details">
    To see the full details of a specific inventory item, click on "**View Details**" in the corresponding row in the table.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
