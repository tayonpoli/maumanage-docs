This section provides a clear, structured **step-by-step guide** for editing existing inventory items within your MauManage application.

## Steps

To edit an inventory item, follow these steps:

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Navigate to Inventory Menu">
    Go to the **Inventory menu** within the MauManage application.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Select Item to Edit">
    Click on "**Edit**" in the row of the inventory item you wish to modify.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Modify Fields">
    Change the necessary **fields** in the inventory item form.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Update Inventory Item">
    Click "**Update**" to submit the form and save the changes to the inventory item.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
