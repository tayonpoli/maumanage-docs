This section provides a clear, structured **step-by-step guide** for creating new inventory items within your MauManage application.

## Steps

To create a new inventory item, follow these steps:

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Navigate to Inventory Menu">
    Go to the **Inventory menu** within the MauManage application.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Initiate New Item Creation">
    Click on the "**Create New Item**" button.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Input Required Fields">
    Enter all the **necessary information** in the fields provided in the form for the new item.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Submit New Item">
    Click "**Create**" to submit the form and add the new item to your inventory.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
