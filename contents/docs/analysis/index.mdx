This section provides a clear, structured **step-by-step guide** for utilizing the AI Analysis feature in your MauManage application. Follow these steps to gain insights and forecast sales trends from your data.

## Steps

To perform AI analysis, follow these steps:

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Navigate to AI Analysis Section">
    Go to the Dashboard menu within the MauManage application.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Run Analysis">
    Click on the "**Ask Analysis**" button to process the data.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Review Insights and Forecasts">
    Examine the **insights and sales forecasts** generated by the AI based on your sales and inventory data.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
