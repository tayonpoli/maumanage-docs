This section provides a clear, structured **step-by-step guide** for deleting expenses from your MauManage application.

## Steps

To delete a expenses, follow these steps:

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Navigate to Expenses Menu">
    Go to the **Expenses menu** within the MauManage application.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Select Expenses to Delete">
    Locate the expenses you wish to delete and click on "**Delete**" in its corresponding row.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem title="Confirm Deletion">
    Confirm the deletion in the pop-up dialog.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
